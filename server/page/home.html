What is OWL WATCH? 🦉

Owl Watch is a smart tab and session manager designed to keep your browsing experience efficient. With auto-grouping, session tracking, and voice commands, you can easily manage your workflow like a pro! 🏆

✨ Key Features

📂 Tab Grouping

Organize open tabs into categories and switch between groups seamlessly.

📝 Session Management

Save and restore entire browsing sessions effortlessly.

🎤 Voice Commands

Hands-free actions with smart voice control.

⚡ Boost Productivity

Reduce distractions and optimize your browsing experience.

🛠 How It Works

  1. 🔹 Install Owl Watch from the Chrome Web Store.
  2. 🔹 Open multiple tabs and let Owl Watch categorize them automatically based on your settings.
  3. 🔹 Manage all open Chrome open windows tabs
  4. 🔹 Use 🎤 voice commands Preset actions
  5. 🔹 Lazy load tabs based on your settings
  6. 🔹 Chose what url is saved and how its saved
  7. 🔹 Save, restore, and manage sessions in one click.
